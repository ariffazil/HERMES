# Shadow Analysis Reference — Institutional Decay Detection

## Calhoun Universe 25 Framework
- **Phase 1 (Strivers)**: Hard work, building, survival focus
- **Phase 2 (Competitors)**: Status games, hierarchy formation, credit争夺
- **Phase 3 (Beautiful Ones)**: Withdrawal from function, grooming over purpose, "zombie productivity"
- **Phase 4 (Death)**: No reproduction, no innovation, system collapse

**Corporate signals:** Rightsizing without growth, farm-outs as "strategy," brain drain, knowledge loss, pressure without direction.

## Acemoglu Extractive vs Inclusive
- **Extractive**: Elite capture, rent-seeking, blocked innovation, short-term extraction over long-term value
- **Inclusive**: Creative destruction, merit-based advancement, innovation rewards
- **PETRONAS current**: Extractive (fiscal drain + internal patronage + blocked innovation)

## Shadow Detection Patterns
1. **Risk Transfer**: Selling problems as "partnerships" (e.g., EnQuest PM6/12 farm-out)
2. **Credit Asymmetry**: Team does work, leader takes credit
3. **Patronage Dependency**: Survival through alignment, not merit
4. **Rationalized Survival**: "For the company" → actually "for my position"
5. **The Beautiful Ones**: Look good, do nothing, survive

## Why Humans Become "Evil"
Not malice. The cycle:
1. **Fear**: System threatens survival (rightsizing, KPI pressure)
2. **Rationalization**: "This is actually strategic..."
3. **Self-Deception**: Believe own narrative
4. **Action**: Execute self-serving move
5. **Reward**: System validates it ("good job optimizing!")
6. **Loop**: Repeat until institutional rot is complete
