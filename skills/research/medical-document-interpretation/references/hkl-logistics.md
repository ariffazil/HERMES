# HKL (Hospital Kuala Lumpur) Logistics

## Key Information

- **Address**: Jalan Pahang, 50586 Kuala Lumpur
- **Type**: Government hospital (heavily subsidised for Malaysian citizens)
- **Registration fee**: RM 1 for citizens with MyKad
- **Ward fee**: RM 3-5 per day (general ward)

## Post-Surgery Care Units

| Unit | Monitoring | Staff Ratio | Visitor Policy |
|---|---|---|---|
| **ICU** | Continuous invasive monitoring | 1:1 nursing | Very restricted, 15-30 min slots |
| **HCU** | High monitoring, non-invasive | 1:2-3 nursing | Limited — 30 min slots, 1-2 visitors |
| **Wad Biasa** | Regular monitoring | 1:10-15 nursing | More flexible, standard visiting hours |

## Private Duty Nurse (Jaga Dalam Wad)

For family members who cannot be present 24/7 in the ward:

### Rates (2026 estimates)

| Type | 12hr Day | 12hr Night | 24hr |
|---|---|---|---|
| Registered Nurse | RM 180-250 | RM 200-280 | RM 300-400 |
| Nursing Aide / Caregiver | RM 120-180 | RM 140-200 | RM 220-320 |

### How to Arrange

1. **Tanya nurse station** — they have contacts of nurses who regularly do private duty at HKL
2. **Agency**: Private Nurses Caregivers — 011-6196-3941 (covers HKL area)
3. **Apps**: HomeCareApps — from ~RM 120 per home nursing visit

### Tips

- Inform nurse station before private nurse starts duty
- Night shift usually more expensive
- Post-laparotomy recovery: budget 5-10 days ward stay
- Specify patient condition when booking: "post-laparotomy, 67yo female"

## Patient Belongings Checklist

Items to prepare when patient moves from HCU to general ward:
- Front-button clothing (easy with IV drip)
- Small towel
- Socks (hospital is cold)
- Phone charger + power bank
- Wet wipes, tissues
- Comb, lip balm (dry lips from NPO + NG tube)
- Slippers (for mobilisation/walking)
