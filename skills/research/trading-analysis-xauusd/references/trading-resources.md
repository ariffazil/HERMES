## SC Malaysia — Regulated Brokers

### Regulatory Context
- **Securities Commission (SC) Malaysia** regulates capital markets (saham, derivatives, CFDs on shares/indices)
- **Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM)** has jurisdiction over forex spot trading
- SC-licensed brokers must hold a **Capital Markets Services Licence (CMSL)**
- Minimum paid-up capital: RM10 juta
- Leverage cap for retail: **1:50**
- Client funds must be segregated
- Most popular international forex brokers (IC Markets, Pepperstone, etc.) are NOT SC-registered but legal to use (regulated by FCA/ASIC/CySEC)

### Tax Notes
- Capital gains from forex trading generally tax-free in Malaysia (no CGT)
- If forex is primary income → progressive tax 0-30%
- Non-residents: flat 28%

## DSIP (Delta Sleep-Inducing Peptide)

⚠️ **Not financial. Medical research context only.**

- Naturally occurring neuropeptide
- Promotes delta wave sleep, modulates cortisol
- Research suggests potential for withdrawal support (opioid/stimulant)
- Non-addictive, not a benzo
- Administration: subcutaneous injection or nasal spray
- Half-life short — timing matters
- **Status**: Research chemical. Not FDA approved. Used in biohacking/peptide therapy communities in gray area.
- **Doctor consultation required before any use.**
