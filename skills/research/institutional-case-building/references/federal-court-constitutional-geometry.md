## Three-Law Stack

Sarawak challenges THREE federal petroleum laws (not just PDA):
1. **Petroleum Development Act 1974** — vests exclusive petroleum rights in PETRONAS
2. **Continental Shelf Act 1966** — federal jurisdiction over continental shelf
3. **Petroleum Mining Act 1966** — federal regulation of petroleum mining

CSA 1966 + PMA 1966 were originally Malaya-only, extended to Sarawak during 1969 Emergency. Emergency annulled Dec 2011 → under Art 150(7), should have lapsed June 2012. This is the **Emergency-lapse argument** — nuclear if accepted.

## The Hidden Weapon: OIC 1954

Sarawak (Alteration of Boundaries) Order in Council 1954 — UK colonial instrument extending Sarawak boundaries to continental shelf. Every Sarawak law's offshore claim traces here. PETRONAS prayer (e) seeks to void ANY law relying on OIC 1954. Full sweep.
