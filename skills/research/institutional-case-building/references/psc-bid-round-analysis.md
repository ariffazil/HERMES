# PSC & Bid Round Analysis Pattern

How to trace operator participation in petroleum licensing rounds over time.

## Data Sources

1. **Petronas MPM media releases** — petronas.com/mpm/media/media-releases/
2. **OE Digital / Upstream Online** — industry coverage of PSC awards
3. **The Edge Malaysia** — business/financial angle on bid rounds
4. **Ad Terra Consultancy** — detailed block descriptions, fiscal terms, bid deadlines
5. **World Oil / Offshore Magazine** — international coverage
6. **Social media** — Petronas Facebook, LinkedIn posts from industry analysts

## Methodology

### Step 1: Get the offering
Search "[year] Malaysia Bid Round MBR Petronas" — find blocks offered, basins, fiscal terms.

### Step 2: Get the awards
Search "Petronas awards PSC Malaysia Bid Round [year]" — find who won which blocks.

### Step 3: Map operator pattern
Build table:
| Year | Round | Operator Activity | Blocks | Context |

### Step 4: Cross-reference with disputes
Overlay with any active litigation, regulatory changes, or governance disputes.

### Step 5: Check if absence is choice or constraint
Key question: Did operator NOT bid, or were relevant blocks NOT OFFERED?
- If operator's core geography has no blocks in offering → absence is structural, not choice
- Check operator's historical footprint against offering geography

## Common Pitfalls

- **"Company X didn't bid"** ≠ "Company X chose not to bid" — check if blocks were offered in their area
- **MBR scope = MPM decision** — block allocation reflects Petronas/MPM strategy, not just market demand
- **Sarawak blocks require special attention** — PDA vs state law question affects who can sign PSCs
- **DRO clusters ≠ exploration blocks** — different fiscal terms (SFA vs EPT PSC), different risk profile
- **Consortium awards** — operator may appear as minority partner, not operator

## Malaysian Upstream Key Terms

- **PSC** — Production Sharing Contract
- **MBR** — Malaysia Bid Round (annual)
- **MPM** — Malaysia Petroleum Management (Petronas division managing upstream)
- **EPT** — Enhanced Profitability Terms (shallow water exploration PSC)
- **SFA** — Small Field Asset PSC (for DRO clusters)
- **DRO** — Discovered Resource Opportunities
- **PCSB** — PETRONAS Carigali Sdn Bhd
- **PSEP** — Petroleum Sarawak Exploration & Production
- **EPMV** — E&P Malaysia Venture Sdn Bhd
