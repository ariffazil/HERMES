## 6. CRITICAL GAPS — the spine
The gaps list is more valuable than the filled cells. The agent/person reviewing will fill these from primary sources:

- [ ] Slot coordinates & water depth (precise)
- [ ] Tow vessel names + master
- [ ] Spud date (real, not target)
- [ ] TD reached + formation tops
- [ ] HC shows? Gas peak? Mud weight window?
- [ ] DST intervals + test results
- [ ] H₂S / CO₂ observed?
- [ ] NPT breakdown (weather, equipment, operational)
- [ ] Final status: P&A date or suspended for re-entry

## 7. EPISTEMIC LEGEND
- **OBS** — directly observed, public source, cited
- **DER** — derived from physics/math, or from OBS via computation
- **INT** — interpreted; model-dependent
- **SPEC** — speculated; **MUST be tagged as unverified**

## 8. DELIVERY DISCIPLINE
- Empty cells stay empty. Do not invent.
- `[Freddy to lock]` markers keep accountability on the human.
- The summary's value is in what *only the field engineer knows* — not in the public framing.
- Before delivery, run a "would I sign my name to this?" check. If any SPEC cell reads as fact, downgrade.
