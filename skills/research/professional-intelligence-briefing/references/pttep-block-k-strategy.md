# PTTEP Block K Strategy — Intelligence Bank

> Compiled 2026-07-07. Sources: Upstream Online, RigZone, NewVision, industry press.

## Block K Overview

- Location: Deepwater, offshore Sabah
- Fields: Kikeh (Malaysia's first deepwater oil, 2007), Siakap North-Petai (SNP), Gumusut-Kakap (GK)
- Operator: PTTEP Sabah Oil Limited (acquired from Murphy Oil 2019, ~$2.1B)
- Partners: Petronas Carigali, PT Pertamina Malaysia E&P
- Water depth: ~1,320m average

## Production Profile (OBS)

- Q1 2025: ~8,939 bpd oil + 4 MMcmd gas
- Original FPSO capacity: 120,000 bpd oil + 150 MMcfd gas
- **Decline >90%** from original design capacity
- Recoverable reserves: 400–700 MMboe (original estimate)

## FPSO Replacement (MAJOR SIGNAL — May 2026)

- Existing FPSO: hull built **1974**, converted 2007, owned by MISC/SBM JV (51/49)
- Charter: 6-year extension Jan 2022 → Jan 2028
- **Replacement FPSO**: Shapoorji Pallonji Energy + MTC won NoA
- New capacity: **40,000–46,000 bpd oil + 90 MMcfd gas** (vs 120,000/150 original)
- Smaller vessel — converted FPSO, not newbuild
- Bid process had delays (Upstream reported uncertainty around bid process)

### What FPSO Downsizing Means (INT)

- PTTEP has **resized expectations** — remaining reserves don't justify original capacity
- NOT "optimization" — it's **capitulation to decline curve reality**
- But still committing USD for new FPSO = value remains, just at lower level
- Murphy Oil planned aggressive expansion; PTTEP is in **life-extension mode**

## Block H Gas Expansion (Strategic Pivot)

- Fields: Alum, Bemban, Permai (deepwater gas)
- Tieback to PFLNG Dua (1,100m water depth)
- Phase 1A: Rotan + Buluh → 270 MMcfd for ~10 years
- Partners: Petronas Carigali + PT Pertamina
- **This is where PTTEP is spending big** — gas monetization more predictable than oil decline

## PTTEP Malaysia Portfolio

- Block K (Kikeh, SNP, GK) — oil, mature, declining
- Block H (Rotan, Buluh, Alum, Bemban, Permai) — gas, expanding
- SK309, SK311 — exploration
- SK314A — in progress
- SK405B — exploration

## PTTEP Strategy Read (INT)

1. **Capital pivot from Block K oil → Block H gas** — gas more predictable, PFLNG monetization proven
2. **FPSO replacement sized for decline** — 40-46K bpd not 120K = realistic remaining reserves
3. **Subsea infrastructure investment** — Kikeh 3B, gas lift = trying to maximize recovery from existing accumulations
4. **Geological studies for future exploration** — still evaluating undrilled prospects on Block K
5. **Operator DNA shift**: Murphy = aggressive explorer → PTTEP = optimize-and-extend national oil company

## What PETRONAS Side Should Know

1. **Reserves re-estimate**: FPSO downsizing 60% implies reserves estimate has dropped significantly. PTTEP won't share exact numbers — PETRONAS needs independent verification.
2. **PSC clock**: Block K PSC has expiry. If PTTEP extends FPSO to 2028 and PSC is expiring, they must commit exploration wells or surrender. PETRONAS should ensure exploration commitments aren't delayed.
3. **Block H is the growth story**: PTTEP capital allocation shifting to gas. For PETRONAS oversight, Block H is where the action is.
4. **Operator conservatism**: PTTEP's national oil company mindset vs Murphy's aggressive exploration DNA. If exploration is slow, it's operator choice, not geological limitation.
5. **Untested potential**: PTTEP conducting geological studies — still undrilled prospects. Are they aligned with PETRONAS exploration strategy for Sabah deepwater?
