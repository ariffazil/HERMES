## Pricing Psychology (Malaysia-Specific)

### RM6 Barrier for Vending
- Above RM6: "boleh pi ZUS la" — customer walks to cafe instead
- Below RM6: "ok la, convenient" — impulse purchase
- Sweet spot: RM4.50-5.99

### Channel Comparison
| Factor | Cafe (ZUS/Gigi) | Vending Machine |
|---|---|---|
| Experience | Sit, wifi, aesthetic | Grab & go, 30 seconds |
| Expectation | Premium, barista-made | Convenient, decent |
| Price tolerance | RM10-15 | RM3-6 |
| Decision trigger | "I deserve this" | "I need caffeine NOW" |
| Payment feel | Cashless, painless | Cash/ewallet — price visible |

### Rule of Thumb
Vending price = 40-50% of cafe equivalent.

## Additional Recommendations
1. **Bundle deal**: "Coffee + Teh Tarik = RM8.90" (save RM1)
2. **Cashless first**: TouchNGo/GrabPay/DuitNow for Shah Alam office crowd
3. **Location premium**: Lobby/pantry floor office tower (i-City, Section 13, Glenmarie) = captive audience, can charge +10%
4. **Volume > margin**: Vending business model = high volume, moderate margin. 80-100 cups/day break even for office location
