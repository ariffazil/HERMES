## Megah-1 Discovery (OBS)

- **Block:** 3K, ultra-deepwater offshore Sabah
- **Basin:** Layang Layang Basin
- **TD:** 4.7 km
- **Reserves:** 200-300 MMboe (initial estimate)
- **Reservoir:** Oligocene carbonates — significant oil column
- **Year:** 2025 — Malaysia's largest find in 20+ years
- **Operator:** PETRONAS Carigali + TotalEnergies joining
