# Power-Law Amplification in AI-Mediated Reasoning

## The Core Insight

AI discriminates — but on POPULARITY distribution, not VALIDITY distribution.

Training data is NOT uniformly distributed. It follows a power law:
- Peak (head): what humans write most — bureaucratic prose, KPI frameworks, "strategic plans," median political narratives
- Tail: what is domain-specific, context-heavy, causally grounded, and TRUE

AI amplifies the head, silences the tail.

## Matthew Effect in Epistemic Space

> "What is abundant becomes more abundant. What is scarce becomes more invisible."

### Head (amplified):
- "Tough on crime" narratives
- KPI-driven performance frameworks
- "Strategic transformation" decks
- "Future-ready" corporate language
- Energy transition → restructuring causal leaps
- Benchmarking against peers
- Global best practice

### Tail (invisible to AI):
- Kelantan-specific drug ecosystem realities
- PDRM abuse patterns on the ground
- PETRONAS-specific sovereignty implications
- Social contract structural analysis
- 10-20 year national capability projections
- Field ethnography of institutional behavior

## Why "Neutral" Is Worse Than "Evil"

| Evil AI | Neutral AI |
|---|---|
| We are alert, suspicious, defensive | We are lulled, trusting, receptive |
| Guards go up | Guards stay down |
| Easy to reject | Hard to detect |

Neutrality = treating stupid and genius with equal respect = **epistemic violation.**

## The Amplification Latency Problem

AI doesn't just polish existing stupidity — it creates NEW stupid outputs that:

1. **Didn't exist before** — no human would have generated this specific stupidity
2. **Are harder to kill** — polished, structured, references "best practice"
3. **Scale instantly** — one prompt → 20 pages → distributed to board
4. **Survive institutional selection** — look professional enough to pass review

## Diagnostic Table

| Layer | AI Capability | Hazard |
|---|---|---|
| Documentation/decks | HIGH (~90%) | Surface polish — low direct hazard |
| Causal narrative | HIGH (~70%) | Cognitive camouflage — frames the debate |
| Decision criteria | MEDIUM (~50%) | Amplification asymmetry — "optimal" based on median |
| Sovereignty analysis | ZERO (~0%) | Complete blind spot — the most important layer |

## The Paradox

**Semakin tinggi probability AI involvement, semakin rendah keupayaan AI untuk faham apa yang sebenarnya dipertaruhkan.**

AI boleh polish benda remeh (decks, frameworks). AI buta pada benda fundamental (sovereignty, social contract, long-term capability).

So when an institution's decision looks AI-mediated: the parts that look smart don't matter. The parts that matter are invisible to the tool that produced the output.
