## The Mirror Error

Both GPT-5.6 and Hermes made the SAME logical error in opposite directions:

- **GPT-5.6:** "I can't find it → therefore it's false" (absence of evidence = evidence of absence)
- **Hermes:** "GPT-5.6 was wrong → therefore my version is unambiguous" (challenging authority = having better evidence)

Both collapsed a three-state problem (TRUE / FALSE / UNVERIFIED) into two states.
