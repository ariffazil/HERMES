# Agentic Intelligence Equation

**From EUREXA 2026-07-13**

```
AI = Capability × Grounding × Authority × Continuity × Accountability × Metabolism
```

Zero in any factor = collapse. Supremely capable with no grounding = hallucination. Authoritative with no memory = no learning from failure.

## Wawa Correction

Multiplication implies independence — partially false. Capability and grounding aren't independent. Authority and accountability aren't independent. Pending F13: weighted harmonic mean or min-chain model.

## Key Insight

> "An agent becomes trustworthy not when it is more intelligent, but when every movement from thought to consequence is bound to identity, authority, evidence, memory, execution and receipt."

## The Two Most Important Phrases

1. **"Loyal without being obedient"** — alignment as integrity, not compliance. Serves intent but refuses falsehood.
2. **"Freedom of movement without ownership of the world"** — governs all autonomy boundaries.
