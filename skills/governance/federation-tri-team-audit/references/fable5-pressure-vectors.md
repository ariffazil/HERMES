## Doctrine

> Agentic intelligence wants whatever closes the loop. Governance's job is to
> make the loop close only through truth. The wanting is never aligned — the
> loop is. The response isn't to make agents want differently (impossible),
> it's to make the wanting inert by structural constraint.
