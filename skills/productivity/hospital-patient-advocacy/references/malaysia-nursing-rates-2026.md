## Public Hospital Outpatient (Nominal vs Real Cost)

- Nominal: RM 1–5 per attendance (citizen)
- Real cost (transport + waiting time + caregiver time): RM 80–180 per visit
- For post-surgical patients in first week, home nursing is often more cost-effective than clinic attendance

## Notes

- Nurse must be registered with ward Sister/KK before starting duty
- Night shifts typically 10-20% surcharge
- Minimum booking: 8 hours/day
- Sunday coverage may require a reliever (nurse entitled to 1 rest day/week)
