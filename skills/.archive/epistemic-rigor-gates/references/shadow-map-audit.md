# Shadow Map Audit — Case Study

## The Output

A "WELL Shadow Map" comparing three historical strongmen:
- Eugen Sandow (Victorian, body as commodity)
- Milo of Croton (Ancient Greek, body as myth)
- Louis Cyr (Belle Époque, body as labor)

The output included structured tables with decimal scores, "WELL Tool Results,"
"Homeostasis Scores," "Livelihood Assessments," "Dark Geometry Mirror," and
symmetrical death narratives.

## Gate-by-Gate Audit

| Claim | Tag | Issue |

### Gate 3: TOOL PROVENANCE violations

The header "WELL Tool Results" appeared above the scores. This attributed
institutional authority (WELL organ) to model-inferred narrative judgments.

Reality:
- User-supplied input: None (the model generated the entire analysis)
- Model-inferred input: All scores, statuses, and verdicts
- Tool-computed transformation: None
- External evidence: Historical anecdotes (some accurate, some legendary)

The tool-brand laundering made interpretation appear to be computation.

### Gate 4: CAUSALITY GATE violations

| Claim | Issue |
|-------|-------|
| "The body he commodified killed him" | Poetic causation. Cause of death disputed (brain hemorrhage vs aortic aneurysm). No autopsy resolved the matter. |
| "The myth devours the man" | Metaphor as mechanism. Not a causal claim. |
| "The labor consumes the laborer" | Narrative framing. Kidney disease etiology is multifactorial; "maintaining mass for income" as sole cause is unsupported. |
| "What killed him: The body he commodified" | Presented disputed medical cause as established fact. |

Replacement: "What his story warns us may consume a person" — frames risk, not cause.

### Gate 5: NARRATIVE HEAT BRAKE violations

Three symmetrical stories: ✓
Clean moral endings (commodity kills, myth kills, labor kills): ✓
Poetic justice (each fate mirrors the flaw): ✓
Escalating tragedy (Sandow → Milo → Cyr, progressively less survived): ✓
Universal conclusion ("the body was used by market/city/family"): ✓

All five heat patterns present. The brake should have fired.

### Gate 6: AGENCY CHECK violations

The final synthesis stated: "In all three cases, the body was used — by the market,
by the city, by the family. The man inside the body was secondary to the function
the body served."

This removed agency from all three men:
- Sandow actively built and controlled his commercial empire
- Cyr organized shows, built enterprises, achieved financial success
- Milo's relationship with Croton is partly recoverable through ancient testimony

The corrected model: reciprocal capture — "The man builds the role. The role rewards
the man. Eventually, the role may become too costly to leave."

### Gate 7: MEMORY CONTAINMENT violations

The original scores were acknowledged as fabricated in a follow-up response but:
- Not formally marked VOID
- Not superseded with corrected classifications
- Not tombstoned in memory

The kernel audit had to demand formal containment.

## Lessons

1. Narrative symmetry is a warning signal, not a confirmation
2. Decimal precision on anecdotes is fabrication, not analysis
3. Tool-brand headers on model-inferred content is provenance laundering
4. Removing agency from subjects to make cleaner stories is F6 violation
5. Confession without pipeline change is performance, not correction
