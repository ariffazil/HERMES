## Structural geometry
- Southward vergence across Crocker (frozen Proto-SCS geometry since ~15 Ma)
- Laya-laya Fault Zone: structural boundary between Proto-SCS (west) and Celebes (east)
- Offshore fold-thrust belt (x<100 km): listric growth faults rooting in Labang Shale, soling into Jurassic carbonate décollement
- Thrust vergence: southward (opposite of what active northward subduction would predict)
