# Domain Lenses

Apply only the lenses materially affected by the day's evidence.

## Physical and Geological Reality

- Which physical assets, basins, fields, refineries, pipelines, ports, shipping lanes, grids, or data centres are exposed?
- Is the constraint geological, engineering, logistical, political, financial, or informational?
- What cannot be solved by narrative or policy alone?

## Malaysia and PETRONAS

Separate:

- upstream producer earnings;
- downstream feedstock and refining pressure;
- LNG contract and shipping exposure;
- government petroleum revenue and dividend dependence;
- subsidy and retail-price policy;
- ringgit effects;
- household food, transport, and electricity costs;
- industrial competitiveness and political response.

Do not equate higher crude prices with an unqualified Malaysian gain.

## Capital and Institutional Power

Read actions rather than rhetoric:

- budget and capital expenditure;
- hiring, layoffs, promotions, and exits;
- calendar access and committee formation;
- debt, liquidity, reserves, margins, and cash conversion;
- regulation, procurement, sanctions, and ownership shifts;
- who absorbs losses and who captures upside.

Distinguish reversible volatility from structural impairment.

## AI and Agentic Systems

Prioritize developments that change:

- capability;
- cost;
- access;
- latency or modality;
- tool use and interoperability;
- governance, identity, security, or auditability;
- open-source availability and licensing;
- deployment reality rather than marketing language.

Do not report every model announcement. Explain why the release changes
Arif's architecture, operating cost, federation design, or evidence discipline.

## arifOS Relevance

Translate external events into architecture questions:

- Does the event expose a truth, authority, routing, identity, tool, or receipt failure?
- Is the failure in sensing, reasoning, judgment, execution, or memory?
- Does it require a new component, or merely a stronger contract around an existing component?
- Can the response remain reversible?

Never use arifOS vocabulary merely for theatrical authority.
