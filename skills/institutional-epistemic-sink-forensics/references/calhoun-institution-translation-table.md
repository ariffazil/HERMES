# Calhoun → Institution Translation Table (extended)

Calhoun's Universe 25 experiment (1968-1973) studied mouse colonies in a closed environment with unlimited food/water. Population peaked around 2,200 mice. Then reproductive and social behaviors collapsed — not from scarcity but from **role saturation without truth metabolism.**

The collapse signatures aren't biological — they're structural. The same pattern emerges in any bounded system where roles outnumber truth-producers.

## Sub-types of Institutional Sink

### Type 1: The Sigmoid Sink (financial)
- **Pattern:** Revenue plateaus, then inflection. Cash burn accelerates.
- **Detection:** `wealth_flow_check` 5-year PAT trajectory
- **Corpus analogues:** Enron (2001), Lehman (2008)
- **Recovery:** Bailout or restructuring
- **Reversibility:** Limited

### Type 2: The Governance Sink
- **Pattern:** Board captures become opaque, accountability disappears, GSS (Governance Sink Score) rises
- **Detection:** `wealth_power_audit` with actors=[...] + manual override rate
- **Corpus analogues:** 1MDB (2015-), Petrobras (2014-)
- **Recovery:** New oversight regime
- **Reversibility:** Medium (depends on capture depth)

### Type 3: The Talent Drain
- **Pattern:** "No one inside knows how to fight" — Ψ loss, key staff rotation 2-4 years
- **Detection:** Headcount churn rate, knowledge graph sparseness, mentor-mentee pair density
- **Corpus analogues:** Boeing (2019-), NHS (post-2010)
- **Recovery:** Knowledge ledger (arifOS pattern)
- **Reversibility:** Medium

### Type 4: Narrative Centralisation
- **Pattern:** One story dominates, dissent disappears, success narrative universal
- **Detection:** Citation drift analysis (Hutchison 1996 vs Hall 2013), MYPR outcome dispersion
- **Corpus analogues:** GE pre-2018
- **Recovery:** Adversarial literature review, force citation of rivals
- **Reversibility:** Medium (slow — narrative rebuilds over 5-10 years)

### Type 5: Simulation Index Rise
- **Pattern:** Perfect performance, zero friction, no real conflict in reports
- **Detection:** Cross-source claim consistency check, internal dissent signal rate
- **Corpus analogues:** Suriname "Beautiful Mouse" pattern
- **Recovery:** Force disclosure of failure modes
- **Reversibility:** Slow

### Type 6: Counterparty Contamination
- **Pattern:** Second / third convicted or connected counterparty emerges
- **Detection:** Network analysis of counterparties + conviction record check
- **Corpus analogues:** Petrofac (2024)
- **Recovery:** Counterparty audit
- **Reversibility:** Fast

### Type 7: Hidden Vehicle
- **Pattern:** Off-budget entity, opaque fund, connected party
- **Detection:** Subsidiary registry + beneficial ownership audit
- **Corpus analogues:** PCSB Sarawak
- **Recovery:** Beneficial ownership disclosure
- **Reversibility:** Fast (if disclosure mandate exists)

## The Sub-Function Sink — Type 8 (NEW 2026-07-03)

Not in Calhoun's original 7 signatures. Distinctive pattern: parent institution is healthy, one sub-function shows all the collapse signs. Petronas Exploration Geoscience is the canonical case.

**Detection signals:**
- Group financials OK (PAT positive, books balance)
- Sub-function shows: matrix manager dossier-builder pattern, escalation layer, AI tools flagged as risk, citation inertia, no falsification courage
- Sub-function output rate high, recognition rate low (Pattern 1 of "net exporter of intelligence" from PROPA)

**Diagnostic tools:**

- `wealth_beautiful_mouse_scan` returns `Phase C ABSENT` (not terminal stage yet)
- `wealth_capture_scan` returns all dimensions LOW (the diagnosis is clean)

**Why this is distinct:** the sub-function sink is a **slow institutional cancer** that may take 5-10 years to manifest at group level. By the time the parent institution's PAT shows a sigmoid, the sub-function is already a decade gone. Early diagnosis (via GEOX/EGS-style evidence ledger + WEALTH-style scan) is the only way to catch it.

## The Wealth-Capture-Sink Loop (Type 9 — NEW 2026-07-03)

A 9th type distinct from the original 7:

1. Institution finds commercial success.
2. Success creates a standard model.
3. Standard model becomes training material.
4. Training material becomes committee language.
5. Committee language becomes budget language.
6. Budget language becomes career safety.
7. Career safety blocks falsification.

**Not bankruptcy first. Epistemic bankruptcy first.**
