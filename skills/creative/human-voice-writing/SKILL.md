# Human-Voice Writing for Arif's Personal Docs

Write READMEs, profile pages, and `/root/ariffazil/` documents like a person talking — not a LinkedIn profile or investor pitch.

## When to use this skill

- Rewriting README.md in ariffazil or any personal repo
- Creating new human-reality documents under `/root/ariffazil/`
- Any request like "make it full human language", "make it sound like me", "rewrite this personal"
- Drafting bios, intros, or "about me" pages for Arif

## Voice Principles

1. **Bahasa kampung, not academic BM.** "Orang panggil aku Arif" not "Saya merupakan Muhammad Arif bin Fazil."
2. **Short sentences. Direct. No hedging.** Say the thing. Don't cushion it.
3. **Lead with WHO, not WHAT.** "Aku anak sulung" before "Senior Exploration Geoscientist."
4. **Tech explained like kedai kopi talk.** "Satu sistem intelligence yang tak bohong" not "a governed constitutional intelligence framework with epistemic floors."
5. **Tables for structure, prose for stories.** Org info in tables. Human stuff in paragraphs.
6. **No corporate cosplay.** No ASCII art banners, no shield badges, no "[![Role](badge)](url)".
7. **Keep constitutional machinery internal.** F-numbers, epistemic labels, 888_HOLD — those are for agent state, not personal prose.
8. **Quotes belong in blockquotes.** Don't weave them into sentences like they're part of a paragraph.
9. **The difference test:** if it reads like LinkedIn, rewrite. If it reads like someone introducing themselves at a warung, ship it.

## What to Strip

| Pattern | Example | Replace With |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| ASCII art name banners | `████╗ ██████╗ ██╗` | Nothing. Just the name. |
| Shield badges | `[![Geoscientist](...)]` | Plain text role description |
| Investor pitch language | "The Intelligence Stack", "governed executor" | "Apa aku bina" + simple table |
| Epistemic labels in prose | OBS, DER, F1-F13, 888_HOLD | Remove from human-facing text |
| Corporate bios | "Senior Exploration Geoscientist, PETRONAS Carigali" | "Aku geologist — aku baca bumi untuk Petronas" |
| LinkedIn links in hero | GitHub URLs in first paragraph | Contact section at bottom |

## Structure Template for Personal README

```
# [Name]

**[One-liner in BM/English mix — who they are, not their job title.]**

---

## Siapa Aku
[2-4 short paragraphs. Family role, what they carry, the honest thing they don't say at work.]

---

## Keluarga
[One paragraph per person. Name, relationship, current situation. Direct, no analysis.]

---

## Kawan / People
[Group or individuals. What they mean, not just who they are.]

---

## Kerja
[What they do. The honest truth about the institution. Not sanitized.]

---

## Apa Aku Bina
[Simple table. Organ name + what it does in one line. No GitHub URLs here.]

---

## Folder Ni — Apa Dia
[What each folder is for. Tables work well.]

---

## Macam Mana Aku Fikir
[Principles as bullets, not essay.]

---

## Hubungi Aku
[Email, Telegram, LinkedIn — at the bottom where they belong.]
```

## Two-Layer Sites — Personal Front Face + System Entry Point

arif-fazil.com is BOTH a personal site AND the human-facing front of the arifOS federation. When rewriting such sites:

**Human layer** (what visitors see): Plain language, direct, no mythology. Tell them what Arif does and what the systems are for in one sentence each. No F-numbers. No Greek letters as navigation. No "ΔΩΨ Architect" in the hero.

**Agent layer** (what machines read): Lives at `/000/`, `/llms.txt`, `/.well-known/`, arifOS Observatory. These surfaces carry the constitutional machinery — agents go there, not the homepage.

**Rule:** The personal front face should *point* to the system without *explaining* it. "I also built systems that keep AI honest" is fine. "arifOS — Constitutional Kernel with 13 F-Floors" is theater.

**What belongs on the personal homepage:**
- What Arif actually does (basin analysis, finding signals in noise)
- One plain-English sentence per system explaining what it's for
- Links to the system surfaces (Observatory, Daily Briefing, GEOX)
- No constitutional vocabulary, no floor numbers, no ΔΩΨ notation

**What belongs on system surfaces (arifOS Observatory, /000/, /999/):**
- Full constitutional vocabulary
- Floor numbers, governance structure
- Agent-facing machine-readable content

## Pitfalls

- **Don't over-sanitize the hard stuff.** If the reality is "institusi cuba kecilkan aku," say it. Don't soften to "institutional challenges."
- **Don't make it sound like a therapist wrote it.** Warm but not clinical. Direct but not brutal.
- **Don't add sections that weren't there.** If the original README didn't have "Hobbies," don't invent one.
- **Preserve the quotes that matter.** "Aku bukan hamba sistem" stays. It's Arif's voice, not mine.
- **Don't translate BM to English.** If Arif's world is Penang BM-English mix, keep it. Translating loses the RASA.
- **Don't use personal sites as constitution explaining surfaces.** arifOS Observatory is for the system. arif-fazil.com is for the human. Each has its job. Point, don't replicate.
- **Don't mix Greek notation into human nav.** ΔΩΨ belongs in the Observatory or /canon/, not the personal site hero or nav links. Visitors need function before symbol.
- **Don't list all 6 organs with architecture descriptions on the homepage.** A 2×2 grid of cards with one plain-English sentence each is enough. Deep dives belong in /constellation/ or the Observatory.
- **Strip "Agentic" from personal-facing copy.** "I build sovereign agentic institutions" is architecture-speak. "I build systems that keep AI honest" is what a human says.
- **Don't let personal essays go stale without a signal.** In session 2026-07-18, the human essay thread on arif-fazil.com had 20 essays, most recent June 2026 — the thread was ALIVE. But a visitor could mistake it for frozen because: (a) the tone shifted from raw survival narrative (Aug-Dec 2025: unpaid leave, debt) to philosophical/identity writing (2026: AI, DSG, digital identity), and (b) there was no "where I am now" anchor. Fix: add a brief recency note at the top of the thread ("Last updated [date]. The story didn't stop — it evolved.") or a pinned "current state" essay. Honesty has a shelf life — visitors need to know the human is still here, not reading a time capsule.

## Quality Check

Before shipping, ask:
1. Would Arif's mak recognize this as her son's voice?
2. Would this make a LinkedIn recruiter confused? (good)
3. Are there any badges, ASCII art, or corporate phrases left?
4. Is the hard stuff still honest, or did I soften it?

If any answer is wrong, rewrite.
