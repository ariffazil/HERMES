# Body Language Forensics for Investigative Journalism

## When To Use
When an official corporate photo (handshake, signing ceremony, press conference) tells a different story than the PR copy — the CEO's body language exposes what the text hides. AI-powered somatic analysis transforms a staged PR photo into forensic evidence of power dynamics and emotional investment.

## Proven: SEARAH Signing Photo (2026-07-22)
Photo: Tengku Muhammad Taufik (PETRONAS CEO) handshake with Claudio Descalzi (Eni CEO) at the SEARAH JV signing ceremony.

### Method
1. Source the photo — official PETRONAS/Eni PR photo (undeniable provenance)
2. Run `vision_analyze` with forensic prompt:
   - Handshake dynamics (who's on top, grip type, finger positioning)
   - Facial expressions — Duchenne smile (orbicularis oculi activation) vs performative
   - Body posture (lean direction, chest openness, shoulder orientation)
   - Territorial markers (documents, folders — who holds them, how)
   - Height/dominance cues (chin elevation, spatial claim)
   - Micro-expressions (pride, contempt, fear, surprise)

### Findings
| Signal | Descalzi (Eni) | Taufik (PETRONAS) |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| Smile | Performative diplomat | **Duchenne genuine** — eye crinkle, cheek elevation |
| Chin | Neutral | **Elevated** — classic pride signal |
| Yellow folder | Empty hands | **Gripped to own body** — protective/possessive |
| Handshake | Standard | **Slightly on top** — host/territorial posture |
| Emotional investment | Low — 1000th handshake | **High — genuine happiness** |

### Interpretation
In any negotiation, the party more emotionally invested in the photo is GENERALLY NOT the party that got the better end of the deal. Descalzi cool = veteran who got what he wanted. Taufik genuine-happy = either proud for Malaysia, or personally satisfied.

### MakcikGPT Framing
- Opening: "Press release boleh tipu, tapi badan tak boleh bohong"
- Frame the contrast: Descalzi's diplomat smile vs Taufik's jackpot smile
- Ask the shadow question: "Dia gembira untuk siapa?"
- Always pair with documentary evidence (Companies House filings, press releases, court records)
- Body language is the HOOK — hard evidence is the backbone

## Pitfalls
- Body language is suggestive, never conclusive. Frame as "apa yang badan cakap," not "proof"
- Cultural warmth could explain a smile — don't overinterpret in isolation
- Always back up with hard documentary evidence
- The most powerful reading is the CONTRAST between two people in the same photo
