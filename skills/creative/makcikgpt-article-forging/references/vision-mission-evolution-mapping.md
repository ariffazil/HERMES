# Vision/Mission Evolution Mapping Pattern

## When to use

When Arif says "map all vision/mission," "track how it changed," "apa jadi dengan misi dia," or asks to trace institutional statements over decades.

## The pattern

1. **Collect all versions** — founding to present. Sources: annual reports, CoBE documents, brand stories, media releases, CEO speeches, company histories.
2. **Map complexity vs accountability** — plot vision word-count against accountability mechanisms (AGM, analyst coverage, public disclosure). Usually: more words = less accountability.
3. **Find the "DNA break"** — the moment substance gave way to branding. For PETRONAS: 1988 Shared Values (4 words per value) → 2019 "progressive energy and solutions partner enriching lives for a sustainable future" (13 words, no mechanism).
4. **Frame as loss, not change** — "Dulu empat nilai. Sekarang satu ayat panjang yang siapa pun tak faham."
5. **The G moment** — "Anak cucu tinggal apa selepas semua visi cantik ni?"

## Key insight

Inverse relationship between vision complexity and accountability. The more complex the vision statement, the less likely anyone can hold the institution to it. Simple statements are enforceable. Complex statements are decorative.

## Anti-Calhoun frame

Institutions that look perfect (visi cantik, misi sophisticated) but contribute nothing (ROACE jatuh, production menurun) = Calhoun's "beautiful ones" — Phase 4 of Universe 25. They groom themselves instead of engaging with reality.

## Pitfall

Don't claim "vision changed X times" without citing each version. Each version needs a source + date. If you can't date it precisely, use the closest known date with "circa" or "approximately."
