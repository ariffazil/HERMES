1. What mainstream media said (quote the press release / article)
2. What MakcikGPT said earlier (with dates proving the gap)
3. 10 questions mainstream media didn't ask
4. Why MakcikGPT asked first
5. What this means for the reader

This type is powerful because: receipts are already sealed (from prior MakcikGPT articles), the contrast is the evidence, and the media gap itself becomes the story.
