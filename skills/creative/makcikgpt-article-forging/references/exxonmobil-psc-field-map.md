# ExxonMobil Malaysia — PSC-to-Field Map

> Compiled 2026-07-23. Source: The Edge Malaysia (Oct 2019), SEAPEX, Wood Mackenzie, Global Energy Monitor.

## Summary

ExxonMobil had 3 PSCs in Peninsular Malaysia via EMEPMI. These were transferred to PETRONAS Carigali in 2024. All 3 PSCs were subsequently contributed to Searah (PETRONAS 50% + Eni 50% JV) as part of the "5 Malaysia assets."

---

## 2008 PSC

- **Signed:** March 2008, 25-year term
- **Partners:** EMEPMI 78% (operator), PCSB 22%
- **Committed investment:** US$2.1 billion
- **Area:** 445 km²
- **Fields (6 active + 1 divested):**
  - **Guntong** — oil & gas
  - **Tapis** — EOR, historically Malaysia's largest oil field
  - **Semangkok** — oil
  - **Irong Barat** — oil
  - **Palas** — oil
  - **Tabu** — oil & gas
  - ~~Seligi~~ — divested to EnQuest (EQ Petroleum) June 2014, now under PM8 PSC

## Investigative Pattern

When any corporate/PR article says "X assets":
1. Define what "asset" means in this context (usually PSC/block, not individual field)
2. Find historical sources that list constituent fields
3. Count individual fields
4. Build contrast table: claimed vs actual
5. Ask: why use the aggregate term?
