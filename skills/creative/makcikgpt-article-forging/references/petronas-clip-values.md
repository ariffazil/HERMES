From PETRONAS Code of Conduct and Business Ethics (CoBE 2022), foreword by Tengku Muhammad Taufik:

| Value | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Loyalty** | Loyal to corporation |
| **Integrity** | Honest and upright |
| **Professionalism** | Strive for excellence |
| **Cohesiveness** | United, trust and respect for each other |

## Mission 1988 (Azizan Zainul Abidin era)

Source: The Edge Malaysia, "My Say: The Petronas DNA" by Azam Aris, 8 Feb 2010.

> "A business entity whose main objective is to add value to the country's oil and gas resources and contribute to the well-being of the nation."

## Statement of Purpose 2020 (Tengku Muhammad Taufik era)

Source: PETRONAS Integrated Report 2020, CoBE 2022.

> "A progressive energy and solutions partner enriching lives for a sustainable future."

## Verification Pitfall

When searching for PETRONAS historical figures, search the SOURCE ARTICLE title, not the person's name + role. Example: searching "PETRONAS CEO Rastam" returns wrong people (Rastam Shahrom = RHB Bank CFO). Searching "Petronas DNA Azam Aris The Edge" returns the correct article with verified quotes.
