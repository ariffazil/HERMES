# Contradiction Map Pattern — Investigating Coordinated PR Narratives

> When a corporate entity puts out a coordinated PR campaign, map EVERY contradiction between what they say and what independent sources report. The contradictions are not errors — they're evidence of deliberate framing.

## When To Use

- Corporate/institutional PR campaign detected (multi-slide Threads, influencer posts, coordinated talking points across outlets)
- Official narrative directly contradicts independent analysis (WoodMac, Reuters, industry analysts)
- "Nothing to see here" messaging that's too polished and coordinated

## Method: Cross-Source Contradiction Table

Build a table comparing the PR narrative against independent sources:

| # | What PR Says | What Reality Shows | Independent Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | "Usaha sama" (collaboration) | "Independent company with autonomy" | Company's own international press release |
| 2 | "Kekal milik penuh" | 50% owned by foreign entity, London HQ | Companies House filings |
| 3 | "Hanya aset huluan dipersetujui" | $20B pipeline, $6B RCF | WoodMac, Eni PR |

**Key rule:** Use the entity's OWN international-language materials against their domestic-language narrative. The gap between what they tell international investors and what they tell rakyat IS the story.

## Method: Timeline Alignment Detection

Map the PR timeline against real-world events to expose the PR as reactive:

| Date | Real Event | PR Event |
|------|-----------|----------|
| Feb 2026 | Rightsizing announced | — |
| May 2026 | Asset transfers approved | — |
| Jun 2026 | JV established | — |
| Jul 2026 | Staff rightsizing round 2 | — |
| ~21 Jul 2026 | — | 15-slide Threads: "Semua okay" |

**Key insight:** PR that arrives AFTER bad news (rightsizing, asset transfer) but pretends to be proactive is damage control, not transparency.

## Method: The "Satellite Model" Exit Pattern

When a company uses "partnership" framing but their own website describes a "satellite model" designed for eventual IPO/divestment:

1. Find the company's own description of the model (investor page, strategy page)
2. Find precedent satellites that already IPO'd/listed (Vår Energi → Oslo Børs)
3. Quote their own words: "stake sales confirm market value and free up resources"
4. Frame: "Ini bukan kerjasama. Ini pelan perpisahan."

## Method: Cross-Outlet Omission Table

Different media outlets omit different facts. Map what each outlet leaves out:

| Fact Omitted | PETRONAS PR | Bernama | ATMA Studio | WoodMac |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| UK incorporation | ❌ | ✅ (no explanation) | ❌ | ✅ |
| 50% Eni ownership | Minimized | Minimized | ❌ | ✅ |
| IPO exit strategy | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Staff rightsizing link | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| $6B RCF from intl banks | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

**Key insight:** The pattern of omission reveals the narrative goal. Domestic outlets omit sovereignty concerns. International analysts include them because they're material to investment decisions.
