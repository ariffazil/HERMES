# Organ Boundaries

## arifOS Kernel

Owns constitutional state transitions, identity and authority interpretation, admissibility, judgment, memory promotion law, action gating, and receipt requirements.

Must not become a warehouse of domain compute, a UI, or an unrestricted actuator.

## AAA

Owns state display, task intake, capability discovery, routing visibility, approval queues, model registry presentation, and operator cockpit behavior.

Must not judge, execute, seal, or manufacture domain evidence.

## A-FORGE

Owns controlled engineering mutation, files, code, infrastructure, deployment, rollback, compensation, and execution evidence.

Must not self-authorize, self-judge, or self-seal.

## GEOX

Owns Earth evidence, geoscience computation, interpretation claims, uncertainty, physical measurements, and geological falsification.

Must not convert geological confidence into constitutional approval or capital allocation authority.

## WEALTH

Owns capital state, flows, valuation, risk, incentives, macro conditions, allocation analysis, and institutional resilience.

Must not issue sovereign investment commands or certify Earth facts.

## WELL

Owns human and machine readiness signals, dignity, boundary protection, coupled risk, reliability, fatigue, and metabolic constraints.

Must not diagnose medicine, claim human interiority, or make the final constitutional decision.

## VAULT999

Owns immutable receipts, hashes, replay, lineage, and historical consequence.

Must not reinterpret evidence or issue new policy.

## Future organs

A future organ must own one domain law, expose bounded capabilities, and declare what it does not own. Cross-domain results remain evidence for the kernel, not self-issued verdicts.
